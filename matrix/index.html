Matrix Controls

URL Parameters:

  • ?color=green (hex codes also work: #00ff00)
  • ?bg=black (or hex: #000000)
  • ?fontsize=16 (size in pixels)
  • ?speed=50 (animation delay in ms, lower = faster)
  • ?fade=0a (fade opacity 00-ff, higher = more fade)
  • ?japanese=true/false (enable/disable Japanese chars)

Example: ?color=green&head=white&bg=black&fontsize=18&speed=40&fade=10&japanese=true
